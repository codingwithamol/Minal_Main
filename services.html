Custom Fabrication

High-precision fabrication services for industrial structures and assemblies using stainless steel, carbon steel, and super duplex. We deliver reliable, application-ready components for industries like oil & gas, chemical, and heavy engineering.

CNC & VMC Machining

Advanced CNC and VMC machining for precision-engineered parts. We specialize in close-tolerance machining for complex geometries, ensuring consistency and quality in every batch.

Laser Cutting & CNC Bending

Accurate and clean cutting using high-powered laser systems, paired with CNC press brake bending for precise profiles. Ideal for sheet metal components, enclosures, and support structures.

Welding & Modular Assembly

We offer expert TIG, MIG, and ARC welding along with modular and mechanical assembly of fabricated or machined parts. Our capabilities support complete sub-assemblies and fabrication units that are structurally sound, aligned, and ready for installation.

Heavy Equipment Machining

Expanding into large-format machining, we are equipped to handle heavy-duty components for the oil & gas, infrastructure, and power sectors. Our setup supports high-weight, oversized parts such as flanges, pump housings, motor stools, and custom machined bases.
